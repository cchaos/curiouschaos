The Road to Organic Certification

It's in the Details

The USDA Nation Organic Program (NOP) needed a way to show and teach farm owners the process of becoming Certified Organic and all the advantages that comes with the certification. I focused on readability, ease-of-use, and cross-browser compatibility and capitalized on the natural aspect of the user’s daily life in the design of this interactive video.

But I didn’t forget about the little details like more information overlays and bookmarking features. The transitions for the non-video screens were also tailored to have movement signify to the user fact-based and behavior-based feedback.

The USDA NOP have released the program and is now available for free.
Play The Road Now

Aside from just the typical choice, text, and feedback screens, there were also some custom screens. My favorite of which being the character menu. A lot of hours were spent trying to figure out angles, make the transitions seamless, and have the layout work in all sizes at all ratios.
