Weddings

I love creating wedding websites because they usually allow me to really bring out my sense of style and match it to the couple and their wedding.

Christen and Orlando got married in October near the eastern short of Maryland. I wanted to bring out the essence of the location and season while also showcasing their personalities. The top banner part of the website ended up being the design on the front of their save-the-date postcard.

“IT LOOKS AMAZING, Caroline! Thank you so, so much!!! Truly!”Christen

Marianna and Cliff started out simply wanting a way to add custom PayPal donation buttons for their registry. Instead of adding a complicated link to one of those do-it-yourself wedding websites, I decided to give them a custom, yet simple site tailored to their needs.
