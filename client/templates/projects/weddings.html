Marianna and Cliff started out simply wanting a way to add custom PayPal donation buttons for their registry. Instead of adding a complicated link to one of those do-it-yourself wedding websites, I decided to give them a custom, yet simple site tailored to their needs.

“It looks ridiculously awesome!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!”Marianna

My Favorite Wedding

This was my most challenging project. They say you’re your own worst critic. That definitely holds true for me. I really wanted to create something that represented Darren and I as a couple.

One of the challenges was to create a registry where guests could donate money to certain “funds”. PayPal made this very easy with custom links and secure payment. Even though the buttons aren’t particularly pleasing to look at, using PayPal’s logo signifies some trust how the guest would be sending money.
